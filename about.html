About Me

Hello! I'm Amaia Elysze Agard, also known as Amyszed. I'm from Trinidad and Tobago, a beautiful twin-island country in the Caribbean known for its vibrant culture, Carnival, and beautiful beaches. Music has been a passion of mine for as long as I can remember. Growing up, I found solace and inspiration in melodies and lyrics, and I started my musical journey at a young age. My style is a blend of various genres, and I aim to connect with people through my heartfelt lyrics and unique sound.

In addition to music, I enjoy painting, reading, and exploring new creative outlets. This website is a platform for me to share my journey, updates on my latest projects, and connect with my amazing fans.
